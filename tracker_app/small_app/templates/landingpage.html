    Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide everyone with the tools they need to take control of their financial future and manage their expenses with an App tailored to your unique needs.

    Our Story

    MoneyPilot was born out of a simple idea: financial management shouldn't feel like a chore. Our founder, Engineer Ornelle, experienced firsthand the challenges of balancing a busy school life with the need to stay on top of personal finances. Frustrated with not knowing how to manage her money, she set out to create a solution that was both powerful and easy to use. From that vision, MoneyPilot was created — a finance tracking app designed with real people in mind.
